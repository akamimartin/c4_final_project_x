"Hey Jade, you got everything? My mom baked us a cake and made ham sandwiches," said Kate, holding up a big paper bag.

"Did you bring the rake, tape and the shovel?"

"YUP", said Jade, a tall skinny boy with red hair. Kate looked at Jade's small shovel and old rake with missing teeth at the end.

Shaking her head, Kate showed the map she drew to Jade. "If we go towards Flame End and turn right at the grapevine, we should come to where there is a gape in the rocks and bushes. We have five hours before the waves hide the cave."

With Kate taking two steps to keep pace with Jade's long steps, they left the paved road and headed down the dusty path toward Flame End. "You're sure this is the right way?" said Jade as they came to a split in the road, the dirt road now turned into two dirt roads that looked the same, dusty and bumpy.
